365 * 400 = 146000

# of leap days in 400 years = 97
146000 + 97  = 146097

146097 / 7 = 20871

Yes, the number of weeks in 400 years is an integer value which is 20871.

As the number of weeks is an integer value in 400 years, 
the days of the week repeat in this 400 year. So, the 400 year can be considered as cycle.
There are 400 of an exact day (e.g. 1 January) in this cycle. 
400 could not be divided by 7 (which is kind of days, monday to sunday), so all the days are not equally possible.
